Becoming a Developer

With no background in technology, it was difficult to grasp the language of coding. Becoming a developer isn't easy. It involves hard work, dedication and a commitment to being a lifelong problem-solver and learner. But, what's also important is to back yourself and your coding knowledge. Not knowing something doesn't make you a bad programmer. It doesn't mean you shouldn't be in technology or writing code. Instead, know that you have the skills to solve the problem and that you're part of a global community of programmers
who experience the same challenges as you.
