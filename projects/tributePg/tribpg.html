John of the Cross

My Patron Saint

As a Catholic I had to choose a Saint before I received the sacrament of Confirmation. The goal is to pick the name of a Saint you admire, can relate to, or aspire to be like. This Saint will be bonded to you spiritually and a role model in life. He or she can be the one you turn to for guidance and protection. To be honest I don't know why I choose Saint John of the Cross as my patron Saint for my Confirmaton. Maybe I was drawn to his name, John, that reminder me one of the Apostle. Or perhaps it was the word Cross which associate with Jesus' death. I still wonder about it to this day.
